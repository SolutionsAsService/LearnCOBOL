Welcome to COBOL Learning Hub

COBOL (Common Business-Oriented Language) is a high-level programming language designed for business, finance, and administrative systems. It has been a backbone for enterprise applications since the 1960s and continues to power many critical financial and government systems today.

Why Learn COBOL?

Despite being decades old, COBOL is still widely used in banking, insurance, and government sectors. Learning COBOL can open opportunities in maintaining and modernizing legacy systems.

  • Reliability: Used in mission-critical applications worldwide.
  • Job Demand: Many organizations need COBOL programmers for system maintenance.
  • Structured and Readable: COBOL's syntax is closer to natural language, making it easy to learn.
